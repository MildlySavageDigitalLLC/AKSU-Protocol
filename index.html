Instructions:

  1. Create a Digital Wallet
  2. Copy and Store Wallet Address safely and privately (each wallet address only issued once but you can attain more than one wallet address).
  3. Press "Start Mining" tab to begin mining AK$U. Press the "Stop" Mining tab to halt the mining process...to start back mining press "Start Mining" tab again.
  4. To view your wallet balance press the "View Balance" tab.
  5. To Send AK$U, press the "Send" tab, enter the recipient address, and follow prompts.
  6. To "Load" an existing wallet press the "Load Wallet" tab, enter your wallet address, and follow prompts.
  7. Mining occurs idle in background, no need to refresh screen once mining has began. Refresh after starting the mining process. If screen is refreshed mining will reset to ensure a fresh chain state to resume where leaving off to not skip mining process. Once the 13 minutes mining block is done chain automatically begins new block. If chain state becomes congested, refresh and allow mining process to begin again. Wait until the counter completes and chain state to catch up with mining process and current block state.
